# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-18_

EV Toolkit is a mobile application that helps users estimate electric vehicle and fuel vehicle costs, charging costs, driving range, and manage vehicle profiles.

## Data stored on your device

EV Toolkit stores app settings and vehicle profiles locally on your device.

This may include:
- saved vehicle names and vehicle parameters entered by you;
- calculator default values;
- app settings such as currency, units, and appearance.

This data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to an EV Toolkit server.

## Accounts

EV Toolkit does not require an account.

## Personal data collection

EV Toolkit does not currently collect personal data.

EV Toolkit does not currently use:
- analytics SDKs;
- advertising SDKs;
- cloud sync;
- account-based tracking;
- in-app purchases or subscriptions.

## Feedback by email

If you choose to send feedback from the app, your email app may open a prepared message addressed to EV Toolkit support.

The prepared email may include:
- app name;
- app version;
- build number;
- operating system;
- device model or device identifier.

You can review and edit the email before sending it.

When you send an email, your email address and any information you include in the message will be visible to EV Toolkit support and will be used only to respond to your feedback or diagnose reported issues.

## Data sharing

EV Toolkit does not sell your data.

EV Toolkit does not share locally stored vehicle profiles or calculator values with third parties.

## Children

EV Toolkit is not designed to collect information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if EV Toolkit adds new features, such as analytics, advertising, cloud sync, in-app purchases, or account-based functionality.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be made available through the app and the app store listing.
